Team Phoenix

About Team Phoenix

We are Team Phoenix, representing IIT Patna in different Robotics competition. Rising with passion, precision and perseverance, our team brings together engineers from diverse fields to build next-gen robots and compete at the national and international stage.

-What We Do-

ISRO Rover Challenge

ISRO Rover Challenge

The Robocon and allied clubs of IIT Patna are dedicated to fostering knowledge-sharing and skill development through events like the ISRO Rover Challenge, where participants can witness captivating demonstrations of our cutting-edge projects.

Robot Operating System (ROS)

Robot Operating System (ROS)

We architect and refine ROS-based control systems to allow modular, scalable robotic development.

Aerial Robotics

Aerial Robotics

We design and test autonomous drones and UAVs, working on flight dynamics, computer vision, and real-time control systems to tackle complex aerial tasks.

Workshops

Workshops

Robocon and the allied clubs, including Tinkering Lab, promote skill-building through regular workshops and tech demonstrations.

Design and Simulate

Design and Simulate

We create intricate 3D models and simulations to visualize and iterate ideas with precision before building.

Remote Control Apps

Remote Control Apps

We develop mobile and web apps to remotely control and monitor our robots and test instruments.

Prototype Wild Mechanisms

Prototype Wild Mechanisms

ML OPS

ML OPS

Deploying experimental ML models for novel tasks in robotics and controls.

Circuit Design

Circuit Design

We design and refine our own PCBs each year with focus on performance and upgradability.

CAD

CAD

We use advanced CAD tools to create adaptable, efficient, and upgradable robot designs.

Space Robotics

Space Robotics

Working on novel space robotics solutions, testing motion, communication, and terrain handling systems.

Robotics Exhibition

Robotics Exhibition

We showcase ready-made robots, automation prototypes, and innovative creations, offering visitors an interactive experience with cutting-edge technology.
